# mdeai.co — The Short Version

## What you're actually building

A **search engine**, not a marketplace. You don't own listings. You don't take bookings. You find rentals scattered across Airbnb, FazWaz, Metrocuadrado, and Facebook, clean them with AI, rank them, and send users to the source — earning on affiliate links and leads.

Think **Kayak for Medellín rentals**.

## Your unfair advantage

Three things nobody else does for Medellín:

1. **One search across all sources** (Airbnb + FazWaz + Facebook + local sites)
2. **Scam filter** (critical — Facebook rentals are 30%+ scams)
3. **WhatsApp concierge** (how locals actually communicate)

## How you get listings (no MLS exists)

|Source|Method|Why|
|---|---|---|
|Airbnb|Affiliate deep-links only|Scraping violates TOS, gets you banned|
|FazWaz, Metrocuadrado|Firecrawl scraping|Public listings, lower legal risk|
|Facebook groups|**User paste-in** (browser extension or WhatsApp)|Scraping FB = bans. Let users paste posts, AI extracts.|
|Landlords|Direct WhatsApp submission|Free, highest quality|
